List of items needed

  1. Chicken (or your favorite protein)
  2. Basmati Rice
  3. Yogurt and Spices mix
  4. Onions (lots of them!)
  5. Fresh Coriander & Mint Leaves
  6. Saffron or Food Color

Steps to be followed

  • Wash and soak the basmati rice.
  • Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices.
  • Fry onions till golden brown.
  • Layer rice, chicken, and fried onions in a pot.
  • Pour saffron milk on top for color and aroma.
